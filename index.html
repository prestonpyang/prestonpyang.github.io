Hello! I'm Preston Yang.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at NYU who enjoys working on every aspect of a project, from analyzing the problem to designing, manufacturing, and testing. For me, every stage has taught and changed how I understand the others, like how an early design choice can become a manufacturing constraint or how, while testing, you discover an issue that sets you back to nothing with even more questions. That process is a large part of why I enjoy taking full ownership of projects, since it allows me to learn more and do more.

As for my interests and goals in engineering, I hope to be part of the future of deployable space systems and in-space manufacturing as well as drone/UAVs. However, whatever I am working on, I do not want to simply have been part of the project. I want the effort I put in to be reflected in the quality of the results and in how effectively the team moves forward.
